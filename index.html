Privacy Policy for Browser Buddy

At Browser Buddy, we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy outlines the types of information we collect and how we use it.

Information We Collect

Screenshots: Our extension captures screenshots of the current browser tab every two seconds. These screenshots are only sent to our system when you ask a question and are immediately discarded afterward. No screenshots are stored or shared.

Email Address (Optional): If you choose to provide feedback via our Google Form, we collect your email address solely to respond to your inquiry. This is entirely optional, and your email will not be used for any other purpose.

How We Use the Information

Screenshots: Screenshots are used solely to provide relevant responses to your questions based on the content of the active browser tab. Once the interaction is completed, the screenshot is deleted.

Email Address: Your email address, if submitted, is used only to respond to your feedback or questions. It is not shared with any third parties or used for marketing purposes.

How We Share Information

We do not share your screenshots, email addresses, or any other data with third parties except when required by law or for security purposes (e.g., investigating abuse).

Security Measures

All data transmitted (such as screenshots and email addresses) is encrypted and handled securely. We do not store any screenshots after the interaction ends, and email addresses are stored securely in compliance with applicable laws.
